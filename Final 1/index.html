Bakin' With Blake

Here are three of my favorite easy baking recipes! Hope you enjoy!

Chocolate Chip Cookies

About the Recipe

I skipped the white sugar and went all in with dark brown sugar, which gives these cookies the ideal chewiness and perfect level of sweetness. Paired with the right amount of semisweet chocolate chips, this treat is a definite winner. The recipe makes a lot of cookies, but in my opinion, this is never a problem!

Total Time Active Time Yield
1 hr 15 min 20 min 3 1/2 dozen

Lemon Bars

About the Recipe

It's tough to top a classic lemon bar, but I've done it! zi kept the tender, buttery crust and bright filling of the original, and made a few improvements. For the luscious lemon layer, I employed a unique double-cooking method. Simmering first on the stovetop like lemon curd allows us to add butter, which balances the tart and sweet elements and adds a richer, smoother texture.

Total Time Active Time Yield
3 hr (includes chilling time) 25 min 24 bars

Carrot Cake

About the Recipe

This classic carrot cake is nicely spiced with cinnamon, ginger and nutmeg and has toasted pecans both in the cake and on top. Plumped golden raisins and a full pound of carrots make it nice and moist while buttermilk lends tang and tenderness. The homemade cream-cheese frosting has just the right amount of sweetness.

Total Time Active Time Yield
4 hr 1 hr 10 servings
