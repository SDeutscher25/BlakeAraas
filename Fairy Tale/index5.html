While Goldilocks was fast asleep in the bed she had found, three bears walked in the front door of the cabin. The bears noticed their bowls of porridge had been touched and one of the bowls was entirely gone! They then saw that one of their chairs had been broken. The little bear then went upstairs and suddenly screamed, "There is a girl laying in my bed!" With all of the commotion, Goldilocks awoke and saw a bear standing right in front of her. She then ran downstairs and out of the house. The bears never saw the girl again.
